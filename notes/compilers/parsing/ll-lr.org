** Infix

- Infix Notation

- In-Order Traversal

#+begin_example
  +-------------------+
  | A | B | C | D | E |
  |-------------------|
  | 1 | + | 2 | * | 3 |
  +-------------------+

        +---+
      B | + |
        +---+
          |
       +--+--+
       |     |
     +---+ +---+
   A | 1 | | * | D
     +---+ +---+
             |
          +--+--+
          |     |
        +---+ +---+
      C | 2 | | 3 | E
        +---+ +---+
#+end_example

** Prefix

- Polish Notation
  
- Pre-Order Traversal
  
- Left-Most Derivation
  
- Top Down

#+begin_example
   ---- traversal --->
  +-------------------+
  | A | B | C | D | E |
  |-------------------|
  | + | 1 | * | 2 | 3 |
  +-------------------+
   ---- derivation -->

        +---+
      A | + |
        +---+
          |
       +--+--+
       |     |
     +---+ +---+
   B | 1 | | * | C
     +---+ +---+
             |
          +--+--+
          |     |
        +---+ +---+
      D | 2 | | 3 | E
        +---+ +---+
#+end_example

** Postfix

- Reverse Polish Notation
  
- Post-Order Traversal
  
- Right-Most Derivation
  
- Bottom Up

#+begin_example
   ---- traversal --->
  +-------------------+
  | A | B | C | D | E |
  |-------------------|
  | 1 | 2 | 3 | * | + |
  +-------------------+
   <--- derivation ---

        +---+
      E | + |
        +---+
          |
       +--+--+
       |     |
     +---+ +---+
   A | 1 | | * | D
     +---+ +---+
             |
          +--+--+
          |     |
        +---+ +---+
      B | 2 | | 3 | C
        +---+ +---+
#+end_example
